Hello! I am a postdoc at Software Technology, TU Darmstadt, Germany.

I am interested in programming language theory and practice, and correct and efficient compilers for domain-specific languages using functional programming and strong types. Usually, I write [Lean proofs](https://lean-lang.org) or [Scala programs](https://scala-lang.org).

You can find my email at my [university page](https://www.st.informatik.tu-darmstadt.de/people/david_richter/), or in any of my published papers.
